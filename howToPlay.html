How To Play Craps?

What is Craps?

Craps is a dice game in which players bet on the outcomes of a pair of dice.

Game Rules

The first player who chosen randomly throws a pair of dice. In the first throw if the total of dices is 7 or 11, player wins, continue to throw and earn a point. If total dices is equal to 2, 3 or 12 player loses its queue and lost a point. If the total of dices is equal to 4, 5, 6, 8, 9 or 10 player doesn't lose its queue, continue to throw but does not earn point either. If player can throw the same total in a row or 7, earn a point and continue to throw.

Elimination

If points of a player down to 0, player eliminates.

Game

A player can not have more than 10 or less than 1 points at the start. Each points cost 5$. Player can play alone to earn money without any other player. But still while playing alone if points of player down to 0, game over.
